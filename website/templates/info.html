How does Greencommute work?

1. Use the map to find a Greenride.
2. Scan the QR located on the bike. Once your bike is unlocked and has been paid the journey will begin.
3. Ride in bike lanes or close to the pavement of the road.
4. Once finished, select a zone to park near you, end your journey and lock your bike.

Parking Zone Guide

Grey Zone: This is a no-riding Zone. You cannot ride or park here.
Red Zone: This is a parking Zone. This location is designed for you to park.

Safety
